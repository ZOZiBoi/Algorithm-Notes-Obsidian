The Taylor series of a function \( f(x) \) about \( a \) is given by:
$$
f(x) = f(a) + f'(a)(x-a) + \frac{f''(a)}{2!}(x-a)^2 + \frac{f'''(a)}{3!}(x-a)^3 + \cdots + \frac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!}(x-a)^n + \cdots
$$

Where a is the first value

$$
f(x + h) = f(x) + f'(x)h + f''(x) \frac{h^{2}}{2!} + \dots
$$
